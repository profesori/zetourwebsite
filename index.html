Concept

We don't like to be bored during the traveling. For this reason we invented ZeTour - mobile app which gives you possibility to discover the cities with more fun and engagement

Join a challenge

Download an app, choose your city and join to challenge you like. Each challenge starts at the same time for each participator.

  • Soon in Apple Store

Participate

Each challenge has 6 photos of famous places in the city and your goal is to find and visit these places in reality!

Win

During this time you will disclose puzzles, use tips, visit the most fantastic places and win a real prizes.

Challenge Process

When the challenge starts you get 6 photos which have to be found. As soon you get that place in the real world, you should take a photo of it and automatically send us your location. Then go to the next place.

During the challenge, you can compare results with the other people to get known who is faster or slower than you.
